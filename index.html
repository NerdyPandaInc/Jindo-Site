The most famous Jindo dog is Baekgu, known for her incredible loyalty and journey back to her original owner after being sold to a new home far away.

The story of Baekgu, a Jindo dog who traveled 180 miles to return to her first owner after being sold, became a national sensation in South Korea. Here's a more detailed look at why Baekgu is so well-known:

The Journey: Baekgu, originally raised on Jindo Island, was sold to a new owner in Daejeon, about 300 km (180 miles) away. Return Home: After seven months, Baekgu escaped and returned to her original owner, Park Bok-dan, on Jindo Island, having traveled the long distance on foot. National Sensation: Baekgu's story was widely publicized in South Korea, inspiring books, a TV documentary, and a monument in her hometown. Loyalty and Love: Baekgu's unwavering loyalty to her original owner made her a symbol of the Jindo breed's strong bond with their humans. Other Baekgu: Another Jindo, also named Baekgu, gained attention for mourning his owner for seven days after the owner's death, refusing food during that time
