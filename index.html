The Loyal Warrior Companion of the Polygon blockchain

The most famous Jindo dog is Baekgu, celebrated for her incredible loyalty and her journey back to her original owner after being sold to a distant home.

This legendary tale became a national sensation in South Korea and here’s why:

The Journey: Baekgu, originally raised on Jindo Island, was sold to a new owner in Daejeon, roughly 300 km (180 miles) away.

Return Home: After seven months, Baekgu escaped and traveled the entire way back—on foot—to her original owner, Park Bok-dan, on Jindo Island.

National Sensation: Baekgu's journey inspired books, a TV documentary, and a monument in her hometown.

Loyalty and Love: Her devotion made Baekgu a living symbol of the Jindo breed's extraordinary bond with their humans.

Another Baekgu: In a separate tale, a Jindo by the same name mourned his owner for seven days, refusing food during that time.

In honor of Baekgu’s journey, 180T tokens were created—each trillion representing a mile she traveled to find joy again.

The burn mechanism represents her enduring loyalty, immortalized on the blockchain.
